Rules of the Game "Thimble Frenzy"

Welcome to "Thimble Frenzy: Find the Diamond," the fast-paced and thrilling game of guessing! In this game, you can play against a dealer or take on the role of the dealer yourself. The dealer hides the diamond under one of the three thimbles and shuffles them quickly. Your goal is to carefully follow the movements and pick the right thimble. If you guess correctly, you win! If not, the round ends. When you become the dealer, you hide the diamond and shuffle the thimbles while another player tries to guess.

How to Play

The rules are simple but exciting:

  • The dealer hides the diamond under one of the three thimbles.
  • The thimbles are shuffled quickly, and the player must choose one.
  • If the player guesses correctly, they win the round!
  • If not, the round ends, and it's time to start again.
  • If you become the dealer, you hide the diamond and shuffle the thimbles while another player guesses.
